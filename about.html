About Peoria Junk Co

Peoria Junk Co is a locally owned and operated junk hauling company serving Peoria, Arizona and the surrounding West Valley communities. We started this business with one simple goal: make getting rid of unwanted junk as easy and stress-free as possible.

Whether you're cleaning out a garage, renovating your home, downsizing, or just need a single bulky item picked up, our friendly team shows up on time, gives you an honest price, and does all the heavy lifting.

We're proud to be part of the Peoria community and treat every customer like a neighbor — because most of the time, they are. Learn more about our city at the official City of Peoria, Arizona website.

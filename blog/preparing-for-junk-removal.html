How to Prepare for Your Junk Removal Appointment

A little preparation can make your junk removal appointment faster, smoother, and even more affordable. Here's a simple checklist to follow before our team arrives in Peoria, AZ.

1. Gather Items in One Area

If possible, group the items you want removed in a single accessible location, such as a garage, driveway, or curb. This helps our crew work quickly and efficiently.

2. Provide Clear Access

Make sure pathways, gates, and driveways are clear so our truck and team can get to the items without obstacles.

3. Separate Items You're Keeping

Double-check that everything in the pile is actually meant to go. Once it's loaded onto the truck, it's gone for good!

4. Take Photos for an Accurate Quote

If you're requesting a quote ahead of time, photos of the items help us give you the most accurate price possible before we even arrive.

5. Be Available at the Scheduled Time

Having someone available to point out the items (or grant access) helps the appointment go smoothly from start to finish.

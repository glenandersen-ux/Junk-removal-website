What Happens to Your Junk After Pickup?

One of the biggest concerns people have when getting rid of old items is: "Where does it actually go?" At Peoria Junk Co, we believe in handling every item responsibly. Here's what typically happens after we pick up your junk.

Donation

Furniture, appliances, and other items in good condition are often donated to local charities and thrift stores in the Peoria area, giving them a second life with someone who needs them.

Recycling

Metal, cardboard, electronics, and certain appliance components can be recycled instead of sent to a landfill. We sort these materials and take them to the appropriate recycling facilities.

Proper Disposal

Items that can't be donated or recycled are disposed of at licensed facilities in accordance with local Peoria and Arizona regulations.

Why It Matters

Every item diverted from a landfill helps reduce environmental impact in our community. Choosing a junk removal company that prioritizes donation and recycling is a small choice that makes a big difference.
